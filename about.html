About Us

At C2 Tennis Academy, we are committed to helping each player unlock their full potential. We utilize the latest technologies—like AI analytics and high-definition video replay—to break down player performance and make improvements faster and more effectively. Whether you're working toward a scholarship, college roster spot, or a higher UTR, our personalized training ensures every student sees measurable progress.

Meet Our Coaches

Ricardo Carvajalino

Ricardo brings a wealth of collegiate tennis experience and a strong engineering mindset to the court. As a standout player at the Division II level, he led his team in singles matches played and was recognized multiple times as an ITA Scholar Athlete. Ricardo is passionate about helping players master their technique and elevate their UTR through disciplined training and data-driven feedback.

Zachary Capone

Zachary is a Computer Science major and multi-time ITA Scholar Athlete with an outstanding academic and athletic record. His ability to connect with players and help them build consistent match strategies has made him a valuable mentor to athletes looking to grow their mental game. Zach is deeply committed to tracking progress and developing confidence through match play.

Jacob Capone

Jacob competed at the NCAA Division II level for three years and has coached at several tennis academies. With over two years of hands-on experience training students of all ages, Jacob brings a sharp coaching eye and strong passion for helping players improve rapidly and intelligently.

Every member of our coaching staff has over three years of experience working with players at all levels. Our unified approach focuses on accountability, detailed progress tracking, and consistent match readiness—so every athlete can confidently grow and compete at the next level.
