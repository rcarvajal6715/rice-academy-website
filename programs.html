Our Programs

Junior Development (Ages 6–11)

This program is designed for beginners and young players developing basic tennis skills and coordination.

Schedule: Mondays & Wednesdays, 4:00 PM – 5:30 PM

High Performance Training (Ages 12–18)

For competitive players seeking to improve match play, strategy, and tournament readiness.

Schedule: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 4:30 PM – 6:30 PM

Private Lessons (All Ages)

One-on-one coaching sessions tailored to the player’s specific goals and skill level.

Schedule: By appointment, Monday through Saturday

Adult Clinics (Ages 18+)

Fun and social training sessions for adult players of all levels to improve technique and stay active.

Schedule: Saturdays, 9:00 AM – 10:30 AM

Summer Camps (Ages 7–17)

Week-long camps that focus on technical, physical, and mental aspects of the game. Includes match play and fitness.

Dates: Weekly in June & July
Time: Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Location: All programs are held at Colleyville City Park Tennis Courts, Colleyville, TX.
